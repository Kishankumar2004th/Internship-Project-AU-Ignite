Future Skills

Empowering the next Generation of IT Professional

Our cutting-edge Future Skills Courses help youth to dive into the world of Full-
stack Development,Artificial Intelligence, Data Analytics, and Salesforce – the pillars
of modern technology that drive innovation across industries.

Our courses are meticulously crafted to bridge the gap between ambitious job
seekers and leading IT enterprises. We have tailored our curriculum to address the
prevailing knowledge and educational disparities in the field. We provide hands-on
experience, develop industry-relevant skills, and build a robust foundation in pivotal
IT domains.

Igniting Futures

AU Ignite works towards building a stronger and inclusive India by empowering our
youth to secure their livelihoods.

Under the aegis of AU Small Finance Bank, the programme focuses on providing
marginalised youth with a platform for skill development and employment. This not
only benefits these young individuals but also contributes to the economic growth
of our nation from the grassroots while enhancing social stability.
