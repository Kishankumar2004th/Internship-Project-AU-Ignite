ABOUT US

AU Ignite is an initiative by AU Foundation, the CSR (Corporate Social Responsibility) arm of AU
Small FinanceBank in India. The basic aim is to unlock the potential of youth by equipping them with
skills that are relevant for career progression.

AU Ignite, our flagship programme follows the Hub & Spoke model aligned with NCVET guidelines. The Hub offers
upskilling and reskilling courses such as Full Stack Development, Sales Force & Artificial Intelligence and the
Spoke academies cater to initial level skill development.

AU Ignite Future Skills Academy

In September 2023, AU Ignite Future Skills Academy was launched with an aim to train individuals
aspiring to secure employment within the dynamic IT industry. This academy bridges the gap
between job seekers and IT enterprises, strategically addressing the prevailing knowledge and
educational disparities encountered by emerging IT professionals.

Key Objectives

Provide relevant and practical training to the marginalized section of the youth
across Rajasthan in 4 major disciplines: BFSI, Healthcare, Hospitality & Tourism
and IT&ITeS.These industries have been chosen for their significant growth in
demand and the availability of opportunities for entry-level positions, aligning with
India's rapid economic expansion. The ultimate goal is to ensure 100% placement
opportunities for our trainees.

Prepare youth for a structured work environment by imparting important life skills
like complex problem solving, critical thinking & analysis, creativity & originality,
leadership & social influence and resilience, stress tolerance and flexibility.
